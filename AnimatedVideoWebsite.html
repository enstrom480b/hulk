About

An airport designated by the contracting State in whose territory it is situated as an airport of entry and departure for international air traffic, where the formalities incident to customs, immigration, public health, animal and plant quarantine and similar procedures are carried out. Known consignor For cargo: The originator of property for transportation by air for his own account and who has established business with a regulated agent or air carrier on the basis of criteria detailed in this document. For mail: A known consignor in respect of mail is the originator of mail for transportation by air for his own account and who has established business with a regulated postal authority/administration. Equivalent to known shipper. Landside The area of an airport and buildings to which the non-traveling public has free access. Mail Dispatches of correspondence and other objects tendered by and intended for delivery to postal administrations. Mishandled baggage Checked baggage involuntarily, or inadvertently, separated from passengers and crew. It is subsequently transported in an aircraft and is deemed to be unaccompanied checked baggage for the purpose of security control measures. Movement area
